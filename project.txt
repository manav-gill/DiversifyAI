1. Project Overview

PortfolioPilot is an AI-powered portfolio analysis platform designed to help beginner and intermediate investors understand their investment portfolios. The system analyzes the user's stock investments, identifies sector concentration, calculates diversification and risk scores, and provides AI-driven suggestions for portfolio improvement.

Additionally, the platform allows users to connect with financial advisors if deeper investment guidance is required.

2. Objectives

The main objectives of this platform are:

Help users understand where their money is invested

Analyze portfolio diversification

Identify risk concentration

Provide AI-driven investment insights

Allow users to connect with financial advisors

3. Target Users
Beginner Investors

Users who have started investing but lack knowledge about diversification and risk management.

Intermediate Investors

Users who want to analyze their portfolio performance and optimize asset allocation.

Financial Advisors

Professionals who can connect with users and provide investment consultation.

4.4 AI Portfolio Analysis (DiversifyAI Model)

The DiversifyAI model analyzes portfolio composition.

Analysis Includes
Sector Concentration

Detects over-exposure to certain sectors.

Example insight:

Your portfolio has 45% exposure to the banking sector.

Diversification Score

Example scale:

Score	Interpretation
0–40	Poor diversification
40–70	Moderate diversification
70–100	Well diversified
Risk Score

Estimates the risk level of the portfolio.

Factors considered:

Sector concentration

Stock volatility

Asset allocation

4.5 AI Recommendations

Based on portfolio analysis, the AI suggests improvements.

Examples:

Add stocks from underrepresented sectors

Reduce concentration in risky assets

Include index ETFs or stable sectors

Example suggestion:

Your portfolio lacks exposure to the healthcare sector. Consider adding pharmaceutical stocks to improve diversification.

4.6 Portfolio Health Score

The platform calculates a Portfolio Health Score combining diversification, risk balance, and sector spread.

Example:

Portfolio Health: 74 / 100

Interpretation:

0–40 → Poor portfolio structure

40–70 → Moderate portfolio health

70–100 → Strong diversified portfolio
